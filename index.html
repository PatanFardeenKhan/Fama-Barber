Our Story

About Fama Barber Shop & Beauty Salon

Established in 2020, Fama Barber Shop & Beauty Salon has been dedicated to providing high-quality grooming and beauty services for both men and women. Located in the heart of Kadapa, we combine a modern salon experience with the charm of a traditional barbershop.

We take pride in our attention to detail and personalized service, ensuring every client walks out feeling confident and refreshed. Whether you're looking for a sharp haircut, a soothing facial, or expert beauty care, our skilled team blends timeless techniques with the latest trends to suit your unique style.
